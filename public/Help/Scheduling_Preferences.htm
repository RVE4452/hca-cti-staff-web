Day-of-Week Preferences

To enter your preferred shifts, click your name in the navigation bar and choose Scheduling Preferences. Here you can select your preferred shift pattern and the days of the week you prefer to work or to be off. The default setting is No Preference. 

  • Important Note: Preferences serve as a guide for Schedulers and Staffers when balancing schedules. Entering preferences does not guarantee they will be honored in all circumstances. 

 

Shift Pattern

You can now enter and save your preferred Shift Patterns in addition to your preferred days of the week to be scheduled or not scheduled. Shift patterns are your consecutive shift preferences. There are four options: 

  • No Preference – the default setting
  • Consecutive – you prefer your shifts to be scheduled consecutively.
  • One on / One off – you prefer to have a day off between shifts. 
  • Two on / One off – you prefer no more than two consecutive shifts. 

 

Day of Week

Here you can select the days of week you prefer to work and those days you prefer to be off. The preferences entered here display on the department schedule screen when a scheduler chooses to view employee preferences.
