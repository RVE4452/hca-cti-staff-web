Unavailability

Clicking a blank cell on a future date allows you to request time off or indicate your work availability. 

To enter a date on which you are not available for scheduling, click the date in the calendar view, then click the Unavailability tab in the side panel to indicate whether you would like to work or are unavailable on that date.


  • Note: Unavailability applies to an entire shift. It cannot be entered for a portion of a shift.
