What's New In MyScheduler 3.10.8.0? 

Release Date: 10/11/2022

Surgical Symphony Integration

MyScheduler is an application that allows employees to view scheduled shifts, swap scheduled shifts, and request time off. Surgical Symphony is an application used by operating room (OR) departments to schedule employees and resources for surgical procedures. The integration between these applications allows OR employees in MyScheduler to view their schedules for the schedule period as well as the procedures they will perform on a selected day.

This functionality must be enabled in the Department settings. 

For additional information see: Operating Room Procedures
